Echo Archive

This interactive sound disk allows you to drag musical elements onto a rotating disk. Each track represents a different sound wave (sine, triangle, square).

Instructions:

  • Use the slider to control rotation speed.
  • Press the space bar to pause or play the rotation.
  • Click “Clear All Notes” to reset the composition.
  • Drag colorful notes from the palette onto the disk to compose.

Design your own sonic memory and launch it into the future!
