What we know so far about EVO Slime? Evo slime is a highly adaptable gelatinous organism found in both aquatic and herbaceous habitats. It can change shape and colour and also emits bioluminescence. Its soft, rippled edges have a calming effect, making it popular with humans for its stress-soothing effects
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Evo slime is generally gentle and humans can interact with it by offering food. It responds to gentle drag, but if it is deprived of food for a long period of time, it becomes small and inactive. And if it is overfed, it will explode to several small slimes.
